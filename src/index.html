The Empire

Founded in 2009 in Los Angeles, California, DEL Records has emerged as one of the most influential independent record labels in Musica Mexicana and Regional Mexican Music.

We have been at the forefront of the genre's evolution, championing the movement that's revolutionized Latin music globally. With billions of streams across all platforms and a dedicated global fanbase, DEL Records continues to push boundaries and shape the future of the culture.
