OUR STORY

The Legacy

Pioneering the Sound of a Generation

Founded in 2009 in Los Angeles, California by Angel del Villar, DEL Records was built on a simple premise: to give a massive, undeniable platform to the authentic stories of the streets. We didn't just join the Regional Mexican movement—we actively revolutionized it.

Today, DEL Records stands as a global empire. With billions of streams, sold-out international tours, and a roster of the genre's most influential and viral artists, we control the narrative from the recording studio to the stadium stage. We are an independent powerhouse operating elite divisions in Publishing, Entertainment, and Studios.

"We don't follow trends. We sign the artists who create them. DEL Records is more than a record label; it's a culture."

Angel del Villar Founder & CEO, DEL Records
