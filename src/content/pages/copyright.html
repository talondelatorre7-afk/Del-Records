5. DMCA Compliance

DEL Records respects the intellectual property rights of others and complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). If you believe your copyrighted work has been infringed, please provide:

  • A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized agent

  • Your contact information (address, phone, email)
  • A statement of good faith belief that the use is not authorized
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information is accurate

6. Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was wrongly removed due to a DMCA takedown, you may submit a counter-notification. Please include your contact information, identification of the removed material, a statement under penalty of perjury, and consent to federal court jurisdiction.
